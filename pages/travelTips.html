WHAT TO KNOW BEFORE HIKING?

Hiking in the mountains can be potentially dangerous since the weather in the mountains changes immediately. That’s why it’s very important to be always prepared.But what exactly that means? What things we should know before a hike?

Things to do before hiking

Find a company

Generaly hiking in a group is better, funnier and what’s importarnt safer. The safety is the real reason why is better to hike with friends.

Research trail options

Do some research and choose a trail to hike. Try to find trails on websites, blogs. My recommendation is Switzerland Mobility. Try do research about tracks, how hard it is? how far is it? You need to make sure that you choose a hike thats within the abilities of you and your company.

Dress for the Conditions

Clothing strategies - moisture-wicking and quick-drying. This means choose polyester, nylon or merino wool clothing and avoide cotton, which soaks up moisture. Dress in layers so you can add or remove clothing depends on the weather conditions.

Happy shoes happy hiking

The secret to happy hiking is carrying of your feet,specialy to avoid blisters during your adventures. Wear the right hiking shoes or trail runners that fit you and grip the trail.

Backpack

Every hiker should know how important it is to carry the proper backpack. Hiking backpacks are a vital example of gear you should have on hand. Hiking backpack should be waterproof and flexible, providing maximum protection for your belongings, and should keep all your things safe and secure.

Snacks and water

During the hike, it’s very important to keep eating and drinking. We should always stay hydrated and prepare a proper food. It’s crusial to keep ypur body fueled so that you can keep your body energized. Healthy snakcs always help. Best are bananas, peanut butter, granola, fresh and dry fruits.

Check the weather forecast

There are things you can do before you leave home to maximize the chances of a safe hike. It’s important to check if it’s going to rain or snow and how cold it’s going to be. Then pack clothes to match the weather.
